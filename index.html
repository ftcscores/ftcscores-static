Thank you for seven great seasons of using FTCScores for all of your FTC events! Unfortunately, we are shutting down.

Our developers are focusing on other projects and the amount of time available to devote to FTCScores has dwindled. We have always strived to give you, our users, the absolute best service and experience on game day for each FIRST Tech Challenge event that was using FTCScores. Since we do not want to compromise on that core value, our only option is to shut down. There are other sites that will pick up the ball and run with it and we look forward to seeing what they do.

Our team will open source FTCScores over the course of this season and hope that it will be used as a reference point for the many students and educators out there that aspire to build amazing tools to solve problems in their communities. If you would like to receive notifications and updates about this process or let us know how FTCScores helped you, send us a note at info@ftcscores.com.

Thank you so much to all of you that have been loyal users for the past seven seasons and good luck to all your teams!

Arthur Pachachura, Ehsan Asdar, Gerry Cocco, and Galen Goodreau
